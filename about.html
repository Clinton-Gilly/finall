About Us

Selisco Limited is a vibrant and dynamic company specializing in the provision
of medical equipment, consumables, and supplies. Established as a private limited
company in 2021, Selisco Limited prides itself on its commitment to delivering
top-notch healthcare solutions. We cater to both private and public sectors,
offering a comprehensive range of medical equipment and consumables essential
for operation theaters, intensive care units, and laboratories.

Mission

Our mission at Selisco Limited is clear: to ensure the availability of high-quality
healthcare products while upholding our ethical principles and demonstrating
respect for social and environmental concerns. We are dedicated to meeting
the needs of our customers with integrity and professionalism, striving for
excellence in every aspect of our operations.

Vision

Our vision at Selisco Limited is to emerge as the premier supplier of top-quality
Kenyan medical equipment, not only in Kenya but also across the broader
East African region. We aspire to set the benchmark for excellence in the
industry, continuously innovating and adapting to meet the evolving needs of
healthcare providers and patients alike. With a steadfast commitment to quality
and customer satisfaction, we aim to make a lasting impact on healthcare
delivery in our region.
